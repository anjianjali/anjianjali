Team Krishna

Redefining Education with the Promise of Freedom

A group of young, enthusiastic individuals who dreamt of bettering the lives of children by providing them education has come forward to join the Global Learning XPRIZE Challenge by forming a team named as “Team Krishna” Our vision is to create a child-centric way of learning model by means of self-learning tutorials for the destitute kids. Our initiative “Gurukul” is a mobile application software, being researched, designed and developed by Team Krishna. It is a software which has the ability to autonomously teach and impart preliminary education to children, wherein the children using the software learns to read, write and apply numeracy thereby becoming completely self dependent; so as to further independently explore the huge amount of knowledge present in books, media and across all nature. The software with its "InteliFriend" touch completely befriends a child, interacts with him/her and enriches him/her with quality education. The software is the personification of a virtual fantasy world as always dreamt by child, where nothing but fun, laughter, friendship, kindness and honesty prevails. The software is by all means a virtue to educate someone morally and technically with an aim to help him/her get herself/himself molded into a honest, kind and responsible human being.
